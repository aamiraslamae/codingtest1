How to make Albaik Broast? Secret Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup corn flour
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 kg chicken with skin (prick it with a fork first)
  • 5 cups self-rising flour
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 4 tbsp. oil
  • 1 tsp. ginger powder
  • 1 tbsp. garlic powder
  • 5 tsp. cinnamon powder
  • 2 tbsp. paprika powder
  • 1 tbsp. salt

Preparation:

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the marinade and then pour over chicken.
  2. Leave the chicken to soak and marinade for around 4-6 hours so the flavor seeps through
  3. Mix the corn flour and self-rising flour together in a separate bowl.
  4. Dip the marinated chicken into the beaten eggs mixture and then coat with the flour mix.
  5. Finish this with all the pieces of chicken and let rest for a few minutes.Repeat this step to ensure a thick coating.
  6. Deep fry the chicken on low heat and keep frying until the chicken is golden brown on the outside.
  7. When you serve, you can set dips of your choice with the chicken
